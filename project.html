THE CHALLENGE

Early in my time at General Assembly, I interviewed a classmate and found that a new app could help her to keep track of her diet. She wanted to lose weight but could not reliably track what she ate throughout the day for later review. I profiled her potential use cases and designed an app that would help her to meet her goals with ease and simplicity.

THE PROCESS

I began with a detailed qualitative user interview and wrote a desired feature set based on that information. From there I storyboarded potential use cases and made wireframes and user flows. After several iterations of those I finished up with UI mockups.
